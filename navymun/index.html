NAVYMUN

Diplomacy From Home

NAVYMUN (North American Virtual Youth Model UN) is an online conference dedicated to promoting diplomacy and keeping our youth connected to the world. We strive to provide the best Model UN experience possible to our delegates while also allowing them to continue to practice and develop their skills during winter and summer breaks.

Why NAVYMUN?

Global Connectivity

Accessibility

Development

At NAVYMUN, we aim to connect delegates from all across the world in an online format. This global platform provides the opportunity to discuss insightful topics with delegates from a variety of backgrounds.

The online nature of NAVYMUN as well as the variety of committees we provide ensure that NAVYMUN is accessible to all delegates regardless of previous experience.

NAVYMUN is an excellent opportunity for delegates with varying levels of experience to maintain their skills and passion over the summer, or get a grasp of what MUN is over winter break.
